Agree (Latest Version 1.0.3)

Forces players to read and accept your rules before they can play. A clean chest-style GUI opens on join with a configurable book, Agree button, and Decline button.

  • Auto-opens on every join.
  • Fully configurable title, book lore, button names, and messages.
  • Decline instantly kicks with a custom reason.
  • Optional acceptance logging for audit/peace of mind.
